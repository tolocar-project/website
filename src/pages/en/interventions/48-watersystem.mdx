In June, one of the TOLOCAR vans spent a week with the community of the Zeleni Kruchi (Green Hills) eco-settlement in the Kyiv region. Together, we worked on an irrigation system for the local garden. All these activities are aimed at maintaining comfortable living conditions and local food production with minimal environmental impact.
